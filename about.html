About Our School

Government Primary School Pethgam Wagoora provides foundational education with a focus on literacy, numeracy and holistic development. We emphasize inclusive learning and community participation.

Vision

To transform young learners into confident, curious and capable citizens.

Mission

  • Deliver engaging and inclusive education
  • Ensure foundational literacy and numeracy
  • Promote moral and civic values
